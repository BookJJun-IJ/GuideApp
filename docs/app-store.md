# App Store

The App Store is where you install new apps on your PCS.

## Installing an App

1. Open **App Store** from your dashboard
2. Browse or search for the app you want
3. Click **Install**
4. Wait for it to finish — the app appears on your dashboard

That's it. No accounts to create, no payment, no setup wizards.

## Updating an App

When an update is available, you'll see a notification on the app tile.

1. Open **App Store**
2. Go to the **Updates** tab
3. Click **Update** next to the app

## Removing an App

1. Open **App Store**
2. Find the app you want to remove
3. Click **Uninstall**

> **Good to know:** Your data is usually kept even after uninstalling. If you reinstall the same app later, your data comes back.
