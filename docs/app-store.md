# App Store

The App Store lets you browse, install, and manage apps on your PCS.

## Installing an App

1. Open the **App Store** from your dashboard
2. Browse or search for the app you want
3. Click on the app to see its details
4. Click **Install**
5. Wait for the installation to complete — this may take a few minutes
6. The app will appear as a new tile on your dashboard

## Updating an App

When updates are available:

1. Open the **App Store**
2. Go to the **Installed** tab
3. Apps with available updates will show an **Update** button
4. Click **Update** to install the latest version

> **Note:** Updating an app preserves your data. Your settings and files are stored separately from the app itself.

## Uninstalling an App

1. Right-click the app tile on the dashboard
2. Select **Uninstall**
3. Confirm the removal

> **Important:** Uninstalling an app removes the app but keeps your data in `/DATA/AppData/`. If you reinstall the same app later, your data will still be there.
