# Odoo — Your Own Business Suite

Odoo runs CRM, invoicing, inventory, and project management — all on your PCS.

## Getting Started

1. Open **App Store** and install **Odoo**
2. Open Odoo — you'll see a database creation screen
3. Fill in your company name, email, and password
4. Click **Create Database** and wait a minute

## Installing Modules

Odoo is modular. Install only what you need:

1. Go to **Apps** in the menu bar
2. Search for a module
3. Click **Install**

Popular modules:

| Module | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **CRM** | Track leads and sales |
| **Invoicing** | Create and send invoices |
| **Project** | Manage tasks and projects |
| **Inventory** | Track stock and warehouses |

## Adding Users

1. Go to **Settings > Users & Companies > Users**
2. Click **Create**
3. Set their name, email, and access rights
4. Click **Save**

## Your Data

All Odoo data stays on your PCS. Nothing is sent to any external server.
