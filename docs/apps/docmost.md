# Docmost — Wiki & Docs

Docmost is a self-hosted wiki and documentation platform. Create notes, organize knowledge, and build documentation — like Notion, but running on your own PCS.

## Installing Docmost

1. Open the **App Store**
2. Search for **Docmost**
3. Click **Install**
4. Wait for the installation to complete

## First-Time Setup

1. Open Docmost from your dashboard
2. Create your admin account (name, email, password)
3. You'll be taken to your workspace

## Creating Pages

1. Click **+ New Page** in the sidebar
2. Start typing — Docmost uses a block-based editor
3. Your changes are saved automatically

### Text Formatting

Docmost supports rich text editing:

- **Bold**, *italic*, ~~strikethrough~~
- Headings (H1, H2, H3)
- Bullet lists and numbered lists
- Checklists / to-do lists
- Code blocks with syntax highlighting
- Tables
- Images and file attachments

> **Tip:** Type `/` in the editor to see all available block types.

## Organizing Pages

### Nested Pages

Drag and drop pages in the sidebar to create a hierarchy:

```
My Workspace
├── Personal Notes
│   ├── Daily Journal
│   └── Reading List
├── Work
│   ├── Projects
│   └── Meeting Notes
└── Recipes
```

### Spaces

Spaces help you organize pages into separate areas:

1. Click **+ New Space** in the sidebar
2. Give it a name and icon
3. Add pages to the space

Use spaces to separate different topics — for example, "Personal", "Work", "Recipes".

## Searching

1. Click the **Search** icon in the sidebar (or press `Ctrl+K`)
2. Type your query
3. Results show matching pages with content previews
4. Click a result to open the page

## Sharing Pages

1. Open the page you want to share
2. Click the **Share** button in the top-right corner
3. Toggle **Public Access** on
4. Copy the link and send it to anyone

Public pages can be viewed by anyone with the link — no account needed.

## Collaboration

You can invite other users to your Docmost workspace:

1. Go to **Settings > Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Choose their role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer)

## Exporting Pages

1. Open the page you want to export
2. Click the **...** menu in the top-right
3. Select **Export**
4. Choose your format (Markdown or HTML)

## Data Storage

Docmost stores all your data in a database on your PCS. Your notes and documents never leave your server.
